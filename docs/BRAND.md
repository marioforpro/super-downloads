# Brand Operating System — Super Downloads

> Source of truth for all brand decisions. Treat as fixed constraints unless founder declares a Brand Reset.

---

## 1. Brand Name

**Official brand name:** Super Downloads

Rules:
- Always written as two words: **Super Downloads**
- Technical handles (domains, package names) may use `super-downloads` or `superdownloads`

Forbidden variants:
- SuperDownloads (as public brand name)
- Super Downloads Pro
- Super Downloads App
- SD App

Display name across ALL platforms: **Super Downloads**

**Social handle (decided 2026-06-16):** launch announcement goes out from the personal account **@marioforpro**. No dedicated brand X/Twitter handle exists yet; a future one must NOT use the forbidden one-word `@SuperDownloads`. (Pending founder confirmation.)

## 2. Tagline (Locked)

> The video downloader built for editors.

Rules:
- This tagline is LOCKED
- Must remain consistent across landing, metadata, social bios, and documentation
- Minor grammatical adaptations allowed only when strictly required by UI constraints
- No alternative taglines without explicit founder instruction

---

## 3. Brand Independence

Super Downloads is an **independent brand**.

- NOT a sub-brand of Super Prompts
- NOT part of a brand family (yet)
- Own visual identity, own voice, own channels
- May share operational patterns but NEVER brand elements

---

## 4. Product Positioning

Super Downloads is: **A professional video download tool for content creators and editors**

Key differentiator: **Edit-ready downloads** — every video is optimized for immediate use in Premiere Pro and other NLEs.

NOT:
- a piracy tool
- a generic downloader
- a browser extension
- a cloud service

Category: **Video download tool for editors**

---

## 5. Target Audience

Primary: Video editors and content creators who need to download reference material, assets, or their own content from social platforms.

Secondary: Anyone who needs to save online videos for offline use.

Use cases:
- Editor downloading client reference videos
- Creator saving their own published content
- Research and archival
- Offline viewing

---

## 6. Brand Tone

- **Pragmatic** — tool-first, no fluff
- **Professional** — for people who make things for a living
- **Clean** — minimal, focused, no clutter
- **Confident** — does one thing, does it well

Avoid:
- "Hacker" or piracy-adjacent language
- AI hype / buzzwords
- Meme culture / Gen-Z tone (unless on TikTok specifically)
- Overpromising

---

## 7. Visual Identity

### App Icon (Confirmed)

The Super Downloads icon is a download arrow on a dark navy background. Confirmed by founder.

- **Master SVG:** `icono.svg`
- **Background gradient:** `#162b73` (top) → `#0a163f` (bottom) — deep navy
- **Arrow gradient:** `#d5ebff` (light) → `#8cd0ff` (mid) → `#30a7ff` (bright blue)
- **Corner radius:** rx=240 on 1024 canvas (~23%, iOS squircle proportion)
- **Arrow shadow:** Same path at 28% opacity for depth

### Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Navy dark | `#0a163f` | Background, deep elements |
| Navy mid | `#162b73` | Gradient, secondary bg |
| Blue bright | `#30a7ff` | Primary accent, CTAs |
| Blue light | `#8cd0ff` | Highlights, hover states |
| Blue pale | `#d5ebff` | Subtle accents, text on dark |
| White | `#ffffff` | Text, contrast elements |

### Direction

- Professional, technological, clean
- Blue-dominant palette extending from the app icon
- No playful or casual elements — this is a tool for professionals
